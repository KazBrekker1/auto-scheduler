# Team Scheduling Solution

1. Sign in with your google account
  a. To create an event:
    i. Go to the organizer tab
    ii. Generate a session code, which is unique to the event you will be holding (Automatically added to your clip board)
    iii. Share this session code with your peers
  b. To submit your available times for an event:
    i. Go to the Schedule Tab
    ii. Paste the event's session code in the 'Session Code' Field
    iii. Tick on your available times
    iv. Submit
  c. To view your team's submissions:
    i. Go to the organizer tab
    ii. Paste the Session Code in the 'Session Code' Field
    iii. Press Look up schedule
    iv. The table will be updated with your team members' available times
    v. Press on a time-slot to see who is free in it
